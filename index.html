Birla Arika Gurugram is an exclusive residential development that offers thoughtfully designed 4BHK homes in the heart of Gurugram. Designed with a focus on comfort and sophisticated living, Birla Arika offer year-round comfort, fostering a vibrant community life with cultural celebrations and excellent connectivity. Now this gives Birla Arika an Unfair Advantage!

Birla Arika Gurugram is strategically located in Sector 31, off NH-48, placing it at the centre of convenience and connectivity. Its prime location offers easy access to major business hubs, commercial centres, and key infrastructure in Gurugram and beyond.

Birla Arika Gurugram offers a thoughtfully curated range of world-class amenities designed to enhance your lifestyle. At the heart of the community are 4 exclusive clubhouses# - The Social & Entertainment Club, The Fitness Club, The Sports Club, The Kids' Club along with the landscaped amenities. A standout feature of Birla Arika is The Walk — a dedicated pathway that connects all four clubhouses, creating a sense of community and ensuring easy access to all the premium amenities. Birla Arika offers an array of premium amenities, including a resort-style pool, vitamin D lawn, multimodal track, and several beautifully designed community spaces. These amenities are curated to provide the ultimate living experience in Birla Arika Gurugram by spreading the amenities across the project.

Birla Arika Gurugram spans across 13.275 Acres equivalent to approximately 53722.01 Sq. M., providing ample open spaces, green landscapes, and a serene environment. The expansive layout ensures that every residence enjoys privacy and an elevated living experience within the vibrant community of Birla Arika.

Birla Arika ("Project") is being developed by Birla Estates Private Limited (“Developer”) in phases on the land admeasuring 13.275 acres equivalent to (approx.) 53722.01 sq. mt. out of which Phase I of the Project is being developed on 11.745 acres equivalent to (approx.) 47530.32 sq. mt. forming part of License No. 116 of 2011 granted in favour of Barmalt (India) Private Limited and others for development of group housing colony situated in sector 31-32A, Gurugram, Haryana. The Directorate of Town and Country Planning (DTCP), Haryana has approved change of developer in the favour of the Developer vide Memo No. LC-2512-JE(SK)/2024/28923 dated 13.09.2024. The building plan of the Phase I of the Project is approved by DTCP, Haryana vide memo no ZP-1321/JD(RA)/2024/41278 dated 24.12.2024. The Project shall be consisting of 4 towers and 3 community buildings.
